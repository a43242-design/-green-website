Natural Resources

Natural resources are materials provided by the Earth that humans use to survive and develop. These include water, minerals, forests, and fossil fuels. Resources can be classified as renewable or non-renewable depending on how quickly they replenish.

What is Energy?

Energy is the ability to do work. It powers our homes, transportation, industries, and technology. Common forms of energy include electrical, thermal, chemical, and mechanical energy.

Renewable Energy

Renewable energy comes from sources that naturally replenish, such as sunlight, wind, water, and biomass. These energy sources are cleaner and more sustainable compared to fossil fuels.

  • Solar Energy
  • Wind Energy
  • Hydropower
  • Geothermal Energy

The Future of Energy

The future of energy lies in innovation and sustainability. As the world faces climate change, there is a growing shift towards cleaner energy sources and more efficient technologies.
